# AI Profit System — Whitepaper

A whitepaper exploring how AI fundamentally changes the rules of business survival and profitability.

## Overview

Most businesses fail — 20% within the first year, 50% within five years. The causes are well-documented: no market demand, poor management, competition from larger players, and cash flow collapse.

This whitepaper argues that AI is not merely a productivity tool, but a structural shift that redefines the economics of running a business. It examines:

- **Why traditional businesses fail** — a data-driven autopsy of the five forces that kill companies
- **The three layers of every business** — Perception, Decision, and Execution — and where AI intervenes
- **How AI levels the playing field** — enabling small teams to compete against resource-rich incumbents

## Contents

The whitepaper is written in Chinese and covers:

1. 企业尸检报告 — A statistical breakdown of why businesses die
2. 公司的五座大山 — The five structural cost pressures every business faces
3. AI改变游戏规则 — How AI transforms each layer of the business stack
