## Reader need
"After seeing this diagram, the reader understands that Vibe Reading is a pipeline: define your reading goal, match the book against that goal, prioritize what matters, fetch content, compress it into your own words, and finally test whether you can teach it back. The optional Step 0 is just a bootstrap for zero-base readers."
